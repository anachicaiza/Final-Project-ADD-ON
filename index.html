My favorite writer

Gabriel García Márquez (1927–2014) was a Colombian writer and Nobel laureate, best known for pioneering "magical realism". His masterpiece, "One Hundred Years of Solitude", cemented him as one of the greatest Latin American authors. His works blend reality with fantasy, exploring love, power, and destiny, deeply influenced by his Caribbean heritage and political views.
